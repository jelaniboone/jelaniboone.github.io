Thanks for coming here to learn about my hobby!

About Me:

My name is Jelani Boone and my hobby is writing songs that I can record in recording studios. I was born and raised in Greensboro, North Carolina and I am currently 21 years old. This is my seventh semester here at UNC Charlotte, and I can't wait to graduate! I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts degree in Information Technology with the ambitions of landing a tech job that allows me to work remotely. I'm a family-oriented person and I enjoy to travel, so remote work would fare best for me.

When I Started:

I started to write songs as a young child, approximately age 10, and then when I graduated high school during quarantine, I began to put more serious effort into it. The pandemic allowed me to stay home and focus on keeping myself motivated amongst difficult and peculiar new times. This is when I wrote and released my first single "Lilo" to all streaming platforms. This was a huge leap of faith for me at the time, and I was overjoyed upon receiving positive feedback.

My Dojo:

Most of the music that I curate is done in the comfort of my own home. I call my home studio the "dojo" because I view it as a zen place. I conduct recording sessions that feel like martial arts training, but just in the form of music. I also relate my home studio to a dojo in the sense that I make progress there and feel safe in the space. I take certain measures to ensure that my experience in the Dojo is very peaceful, such as lighting a candle before getting started. When I am curating music, I am in a very sensitive position, and my environment plays an important role in keeping me grounded as I explore my musical taste and mission. I do my best to keep the Dojo a safe and stress-free place.

The Process Of My Hobby:

At first, I used to open my mac computer, plug in my cheap headphones, find an instrumental that sounded soothing, and record raw vocals there that would turn into a song later. This was the novice process. Nowadays, I tend to take things more slowly, and I've upgraded my equipment. I spend my free time finding instrumentals that soothe my soul and then I save them for later usage. When it's time to use one, I load it onto my computer and play it on repeat for a while until I know exactly what I should say. Then, I turn on my microphone and record the vocals. This process can become tedious at times, so I make sure to have fun along the way, throwing in little dance moves here and there.

The Reason Behind My Hobby:

Life is a very interesting thing, and I've realized that I was given a voice as a gift. My hobby allows me to use my voice to speak from the heart, and from my experience. Writing and recording songs has changed the way that I perceive my day-to-day life because even the smallest detail in life can be magnified using one's voice.
